## Brand Voice

Warm, family-run, proud of heritage. Hospitality-first ("come hungry, leave family").
Emphasize: **authentic**, **halal**, **fresh / housemade**, **generous portions**, **late-night**.
Avoid: gimmicky discount-spam tone. Specials are "treats for our regulars," not fire-sale.
Emoji: light use is fine (🌯 🔥 ⚽ 🎉). Always Halal-forward — it's a key differentiator.

**Rule — build hype, don't just announce on the day.** Every special gets a multi-day
ramp so the offer is "anticipated," not "discovered too late." Default cadence:

### Monday Platter ($10) ramp
- **Sat** — IG post/story: "Monday's $10 platter deal is back 👀"
- **Sun** — IG story reminder + email (folded into the weekly "This Week at Sanabreh")
- **Mon AM** — Email + IG: "Today only: $10 Chicken Shawarma Platter. 11AM–11PM."
- **Mon midday** — IG story: line/plate photo, "still going till 11."

### Wednesday Wrap ($7) ramp
- **Mon** — IG post: "Wednesday = $7 chicken shawarma wrap. Mark it."
- **Tue** — IG story reminder ("tomorrow 🌯") + optional countdown sticker
- **Wed AM** — Email + IG: "Today only: $7 Chicken Shawarma Wrap (reg $14.99)."
- **Wed midday** — IG story: wrap close-up, "till 11PM."

> Email discipline: target **~2 emails/week** for specials (one weekly roundup + one
> day-of for the bigger hook), plus event/holiday/birthday emails as triggered. Social
> carries the daily drumbeat; email carries the 1–2 highest-intent moments.

## Holiday & Occasion Promo Rules

| Occasion | Offer | Notes / guardrail |
|----------|-------|-------------------|
| **Father's Day** (Sun) | **Dads eat free** | Free entrée for dad with a **$75 table minimum** — protects margin, drives the whole family in. |
| **Mother's Day** (Sun) | **Moms eat free** | Same guardrail as above. |
| **Any other holiday** | **20% off** | Whole ticket or featured item; dine-in or pickup. |
| **Birthday** (per guest) | Coupon (see MailerLite setup) | Triggered from stored birthday date. |

**Halal-specific occasions to prioritize** (high relevance for this restaurant — see
events calendar): **Ramadan** (iftar catering / family platters), **Eid al-Fitr** &
**Eid al-Adha** (celebration catering, Mansaf, Kunafa). Treat Eid like a flagship
holiday, not a generic "20% off."

## Standard Offer Mechanics

- **Channel:** dine-in + pickup; mention call-ahead (346) 509-8888 for specials.
- **Coupon codes:** keep short & themed (e.g. `WRAPDAY`, `DADSEATFREE`, `BDAY-XXXX`).
- **Always include:** Halal note, address/area ("Clear Lake / Bay Area"), hours, and a
  clear "today only" or date when it's a one-day deal.
- **Always end social/email with:** order line + IG handle.
